Reception

Location

Following the ceremony, the reception will be held across the street at the Strategic Air Command & Aerospace Museum. Guests are invited to take advantage of Ollie the Trolley as a shuttle from the ceremony to the reception location.

Food

Help yourself to a BBQ buffet including Pork Sliders, Gourmet Macaroni & Cheese, Baked Beans, Salad, and a Mashed Potato Bar. Water will be provided free of charge, and beverages other than water can be purchased at the cash bar.

Music

During the reception, please enjoy the live musical stylings of Frankie Lee and the Infernos! There will be plenty of room to dance, so please do! We can't wait to see all your moves.
